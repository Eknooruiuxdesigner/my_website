Coffee Connoisseur

In this UI/UX design case study, we will focus on creating an engaging and user-friendly website for a coffee shop. The website aims to showcase the coffee shop's commitment to quality beans, diverse menu options, and creating a welcoming environment for customers. Our goal is to enhance the overall user experience, making it easy for visitors to explore the coffee offerings, learn about brewing techniques, and find essential information about the coffee shop.

BROS

BROS is a web-based and mobile programme that tracks school buses and provides details on the driver, vehicle, and route. In the event of an accident, it will assist organizations and parents in keeping track. Tracking school and university buses is the major goal of the BROS project. With all the details about the bus, the driver, and the designated route, parents can simply follow the school bus that their children are riding.
