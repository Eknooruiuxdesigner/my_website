Welcome to My Portfolio!

My name is Eknoor Kaur. I've loved design and art since I was a kid. I always analyze the designs around me and enjoy learning about them. It's not just a job for me; it's my passion.

My journey began when my brother introduced me to UI/UX design. I started with Figma and soon learned video editing, branding, and 3D modeling with Blender. My skills in sketching realistic portraits add a special touch to my work.

I've worked on various projects like dashboards, websites, mobile apps, and visual content such as banners and posters. I'm especially proud of my work on projects that help the community. I enjoy new challenges and keep up with the latest design trends and technologies to make sure my work stays fresh and innovative.

Take a look at my portfolio to see some of my favorite projects. Thanks for visiting, and I look forward to working together on future design projects!.
